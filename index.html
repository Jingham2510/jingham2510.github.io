About Me

Currently: Pushing dirt with robots!

I hold a first-class MEng Electronic Engineering degree from the University of York (where I was also awarded best MEng graduate). I previously worked in the RF industry and have come to realise that I have a passion for research and robots! I am currently in the first year of my PhD at the University of Cambridge, working alongside my supervisor Jim Hambleton to solve terraforming problems using robots!
